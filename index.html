The Tale of the King and Princess Eliana

Once upon a time, in a distant kingdom, there was a wise and just king. He ruled with fairness, and by his side was always his wife—the Queen, a mysterious and enigmatic woman. She had the gift of foresight and knew things hidden from ordinary people. As the evening sun bathed the castle in golden hues, the Queen called her daughter, the beautiful Princess Eliana. The girl hurried to her mother's chamber, feeling a sense of foreboding in her heart.

— My daughter, — the Queen said softly, — I know the day of my death, and it is near. But there is one secret you must know...

The princess, frightened, clenched her hands.

— What secret, mother?

— You have inherited my gift, but you have not yet realized it. Tomorrow night, when the clock strikes midnight, you will have a dream: you will be sitting on the throne, but everything around you will be empty. No father, no friends, no servants—only the wind. Soon, a mysterious advisor will appear in the palace, persuading the king to change the laws of the land. But Eliana remembered her dream and began to follow the stranger. Soon, she uncovered the plot against her father and saved the kingdom.
